Biography

I am now a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Computer Science and Technology (SDUCS), Shandong University (SDU), working with Prof. Dr. Shiqing Xin’s team. Prior to that, I received a M.S. in the Fujian Institute of Research on the Structure of Matter, Chinese Academy of Sciences (FJIRSM) and the North University of China (NUC) in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Shengzong Zhou.

I have wide research interests in Geometric Deep Learning, with a focus on Mesh Processing, 3D Shape Segmentation, Object Tracking driven by Deep Learning.
