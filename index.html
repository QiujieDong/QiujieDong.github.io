Biography

I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at The University of Hong Kong (HKU) from Oct. 2024 to Oct. 2025. Prior to that, I received my Ph.D. degree from the Interdisciplinary Research Center (IRC), Shandong University (SDU) in 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Shiqing Xin. I received my Joint-M.Eng. degree from Fujian Institute of Research on the Structure of Matter, Chinese Academy of Sciences (FJIRSM)-North University of China in 2020, advised by Prof. Shengzong Zhou. I am fortunate to work closely with Prof. Wenping Wang and Prof. Changhe Tu.

My research lies at the intersection of Computer Graphics and Deep Learning. More specifically, I focus on geometric deep learning, including quadrilateral mesh generation, point cloud–based surface reconstruction, and mesh feature extraction, among others.

I will soon join the School of Software at Shandong University, and I look forward to this new journey.
