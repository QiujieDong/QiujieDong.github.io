Biography

I am now a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Computer Science and Technology (SDUCS), Shandong University (SDU), under the supervision of Prof. Shiqing Xin. Prior to that, I received the M.S. from the Fujian Institute of Research on the Structure of Matter, Chinese Academy of Sciences (FJIRSM) and the North University of China in 2020, working with Prof. Shengzong Zhou’s team.
